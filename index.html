Mastering and Post for New York City and the Hudson Valley.

Jon Cohrs is an audio engineer and media artist based in Woodstock, NY. For the last 15 years he’s been working as an audio mastering engineer working on records and films with artists like Blind Pilot, Horsefeathers, Rirkrit Tiravanij, Tara Jane O’Neil,Tin Hat, Laurie Anderson, Cory Arcangel, Talkdemonic among others. After 8 years of running Spleenless Mastering in Portland, Oregon and recording at TapeOp Magazine founder, Larry Crane’s, Jackpot! Recording Studio, Jon Cohrs moved to New York City where he moved Spleenless Mastering to Brooklyn, where it gained a reputation for quality, affordability, and most importantly in fostering an interactive relationship with the artists. In 2017, Jon Cohrs opened Bear Call Mastering in Kingston - Woodstock NY, with the same mission but with an improve studio and equipment.
